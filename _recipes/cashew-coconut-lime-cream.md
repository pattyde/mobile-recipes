## What you need

* 1 cup cashews, soaked for 30 minutes in very hot water then drained and rinsed
* 1/4 cup lime juice
* 1/4 cup coconut milk
* 1/4 cup water
* Salt to taste

<!-- break -->

## How it's done

Blend until smooth, adding more water as required.
