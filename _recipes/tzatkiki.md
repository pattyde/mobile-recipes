## What you need

* 500g greek yoghurt
* ½ long English cucumber, deseeded
* 1 tbsp fresh dill
* 2 tbsp lemon juice
* 4 cloves garlic
* ½ tsp garlic powder
* 1 tbsp olive oil
* Salt and pepper to taste

<!-- break -->

## How it's done

Mix all ingredients in food processor until smooth, adjust levels of salt/pepper/garlic/lemon to taste.
