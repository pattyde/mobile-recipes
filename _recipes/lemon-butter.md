## What you need

* 125 g butter
* 4 egg yolks
* Juice of 3 lemons
* 200g sugar
* Rind of 2 lemons (finely grated)

<!-- break -->

## How it's done

Melt butter in double broiler

Beat together yolks, lemon juice and sugar

Add to butter

Cook until thickened

Add lemon rind

Bottle
